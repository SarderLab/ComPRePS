Welcome to ComPRePS: Revolutionizing Digital Pathology with AI

ComPRePS (Computational Renal Pathology Suite) is a state-of-the-art cloud-based image analysis platform that leverages artificial intelligence (AI) to revolutionize digital pathology. Developed under the leadership of Dr. Pinaki Sarder at the University of Florida, ComPRePS aims to make AI-driven histological analysis accessible to all, with a particular focus on renal pathology.

Empowering Pathologists with AI

In today’s era of high-resolution whole-slide microscopy, analyzing intricate tissue structures can be time-consuming and complex. ComPRePS changes this by harnessing advanced deep neural network (DNN)-based AI technologies, enabling pathologists to streamline diagnostics and prognostics. With its computational power and sophisticated algorithms, ComPRePS allows professionals to conduct detailed image analysis quickly and accurately, enhancing their ability to diagnose diseases with precision.

End-to-End Solutions for Precision Medicine

ComPRePS offers an all-in-one solution for renal pathology analysis, designed for both scalability and efficiency. The platform provides on-demand cloud computing, enabling users to process large volumes of data without the need for costly local hardware. With an intuitive web-based interface, pathologists can easily upload, visualize, and interact with their data, facilitating the integration of computational pathology into routine diagnostics and research. This seamless workflow empowers medical professionals to enhance the quality of patient care through data-driven insights.

Fostering Collaboration and Innovation

Built on the NIH’s FAIR Data Principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable), ComPRePS promotes open science and collaboration within the global pathology community. By offering access to cutting-edge AI tools, it enables researchers and clinicians worldwide to work together, sharing insights and improving diagnostic techniques. With ComPRePS, AI-powered image analysis is no longer limited to specialized labs—it’s a tool for everyone, driving innovation and improving outcomes in medical research and patient care.

Try It Out!

Experience the power of ComPRePS by following these simple steps:

  1. Sign Up: Create an account on the Digital Slide Archive (DSA).
  2. Upload Your Image: Log in and upload a Whole Slide Image (WSI) in the "Collections" tab or your personal directory.
  3. Run the Analysis:
    • Open the WSI in HistomicsUI.
    • Select the appropriate plugin (e.g., Multi Compartment Segmentation) from the "Analyses" tab.
    • Choose the base directory and pre-trained model from the model zoo, then click "Submit."
  4. Visualize Results: After processing, view the segmented kidney compartments or structures by selecting layers from the "Annotations" tab.
